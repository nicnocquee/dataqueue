---
title: Job States
description: The different states a job can be in and how they change
---

A job can be in one of these states:

- `pending`: The job is waiting in the queue to be processed.
- `processing`: The job is currently being worked on.
- `completed`: The job finished successfully.
- `failed`: The job did not finish successfully. It can be retried up to `max_attempts` times.
- `cancelled`: The job was cancelled before it finished.
- `retried`: The job is being tried again after a failure.
